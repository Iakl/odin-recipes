Biscochon

Easy and Tasty Huge Biscochon

A biscochon can be made as easy as following the 1-2-3-4 rule;

Ingredients

  • 1 big coup of yogurt
  • 2 big coups of sugar
  • 3 big coups of flavour
  • 4 couples of eggs
  • a couple of tablespoons of baking powder

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven at 180 celcius.
  2. In a bowl, add the yougurt with the eggs and the sugar and mix them until you get an homogenous consistency.
  3. Add the flavour and the baking powder and continue mixing.
  4. Put the mix in a oven recipient and put it into the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  5. Voila.
